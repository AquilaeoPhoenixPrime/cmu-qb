Events at Carnegie Mellon University

Mellon Bowl
Carnegie Mellon Quiz Bowl would like to cordially invite your school to attend Mellon Bowl XVI, an introductory quiz bowl tournament to be held on our Pittsburgh campus on Saturday, October 27, 2018.
Divisions: This tournament will have 2 divisions. Eligibility for Division II, the new-to-quizbowl division, is as follows. The school sponsoring the team must have never played a pyramidal Quizbowl tournament, or not done so in at least 3 years. Schools with established quizbowl programs may enter Division II teams, provided that they are comprised of players in their first tournament.
Note that “pyramidal quizbowl,” in general, denotes any tournament that has used questions supplied by National Academic Quiz Tournaments (NAQT). Recent participation in local championships, Hometown High-Q, and History Bowl does not count against a team’s eligibility for Division II. If you have any questions about eligibility, please feel free to email us at cmuquizbowl@gmail.com. If your school does not qualify for Division II, your team must register in Division I.
Because this is primarily an introductory and fun quizbowl tournament, there will likely be no nationals qualification at this tournament. We encourage teams aiming to qualify for nationals to also attend Pitt’s SAGACITY tournament on November 17.
Questions: This tournament will use the PSACA 2 novice set. You can see some sample rounds with questions of the same format and difficulty here.
Format: If you are unfamiliar, you can read about the rules of quizbowl at this article. This tournament will include both powers and negs.
Field Size: here is an initial soft cap of 16 teams in Division I and 10 teams in Division II, with potential to expand to 20 and 12, pending staff. Registration is first-come, first-serve, although we will keep a waiting-list.
Registration: To register, please email our team at cmuquizbowl@gmail.com with the number of teams, buzzers, and volunteer staff you plan to bring. Registration will close on Friday, October 19.
Fees and Discounts:
Condition Fee/Discount
First team $75
Additional teams $65/team
Buzzer system -$10/system *
New to Quizbowl -$20/team
Volunteer staff -$15/staffer *
* Buzzers must be completely functional to receive discount; moderators should be experienced.
Minimum fee per team is $40.
Payment: Please make checks out to "Carnegie Mellon University". We prefer to be paid with cash or check on the day of the tournament; please email if you need to make other arrangements.
Schedule: Day-of registration will be from 8:30 to 9:00 am, with round 1 starting by 9:30. Number of rounds and playoff/finals format will be determined by the field size. Logistical information will be provided to registered teams.
IMPORTANT: We take safety at this tournament extremely seriously, and all staffers at this tournament have been background-cleared at the state and federal level. Under PA Act 153 regulations, all students participating in this tournament MUST present a signed waiver from CMU's Child Protection Services in order to play. We will email a PDF of this waiver to each team upon registration. If students are under 18 years of age, a parent must cosign. If you have any questions, please direct them to cmuquizbowl@gmail.com and we will get back to you soon!
