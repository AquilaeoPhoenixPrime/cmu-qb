State College wins Mellon Bowl XIII

October 31, 2015
Twenty-four teams competed at the thirteenth annual Mellon Bowl on Saturday, with State College A winning their second consecutive title, and Winchester Thurston and Alagar Homeschool finishing in a tie for second.
