    About Us

    Carnegie Mellon Quiz Bowl is a team of academic competitors who contend against universities across the country. We also host quizbowl tournaments for local high schools.

    What is quizbowl?

    Quizbowl is an academic competition featuring two teams of four players each, which use buzzers to determine who answers a question first.
