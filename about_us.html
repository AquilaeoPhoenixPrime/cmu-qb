About Us

Carnegie Mellon Quiz Bowl, established in 1993, is a team of more than 20 life-long learners who compete in academic competitions against similar teams across the country. In the past, we competed at Ohio State University, Penn State University, University of Pennsylvania, and Johns Hopkins University, in addition to several national tournaments in Chicago and one in Boston. Specifically, we play quiz bowl, and if you don't know what that is, check out the link below!

In addition to competing, we also host two high school quiz bowl tournaments per year: Mellon Bowl at the end of October, and Carnegie Quiz at the end of February or the beginning of March. By doing this, we encourage learning both in and beyond the classroom. We accomplish this by rewarding their knowledge through exhilarating, fast-paced competition and connecting them with bright students from other schools. Feel free to contact us at any time!
